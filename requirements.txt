# tifffile     -> multi-channel TIFF export (alpha) in Super Saver.
#                 Without tifffile: fallback to PIL (no multi-channel alpha).
# nvidia-ml-py -> global GPU VRAM monitoring (including external processes like Ollama) in PyCode Max.
#                 Without nvidia-ml-py: gpu["vram"]() gracefully falls back to {"available": False}.

scipy
tifffile
nvidia-ml-py
